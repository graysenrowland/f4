Primary & Character Arcs
Primary Story Arc — What changes from beginning to end?

Positive Change

The character grows, repents, learns, or becomes better.

Negative Change

The character declines, falls, hardens, or chooses wrongly.

Positive Steadfast

The character holds to what is right and changes others.

Negative Steadfast

The character refuses needed change and remains wrong.
