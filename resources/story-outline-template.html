F₄ Story Development
Story Outline Workbook
Brainstorm scenes using the 14 Hero’s Journey beats before building your scene list.

How to use this workbook

This workbook comes before your scene list and screenplay. Use it while your imagination is still wide open. Each beat helps you brainstorm possible scenes, choices, conflicts, surprises, images, and character changes. Later, choose the strongest ideas and arrange them into a scene list.

Story Title

Team / Writer

Example

Beat: Call to Adventure
Possible moment: A problem arrives that the hero can no longer ignore.

Remember

  • One step may use several scenes.
  • One scene may serve more than one step.
  • Some ideas may disappear later.
  • Focus on what the audience can see and hear.
