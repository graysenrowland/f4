F₄ Story Development
Story Outline Workbook
Turn Hero’s Journey beats into filmable scenes.

How to use this workbook

Start with your Writing Worksheet. Then use each page here to decide what scenes will show that story beat on screen. You are not writing screenplay pages yet. You are building the bridge between story structure and a screenplay draft.

Story Title

Team / Writer

Example

Beat: Call to Adventure
Scene: A problem arrives that the hero can no longer ignore.

Remember

  • One step may use several scenes.
  • One scene may serve more than one step.
  • Focus on what the audience can see and hear.
