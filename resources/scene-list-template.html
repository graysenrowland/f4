Scene List Template

Use this after the Story Outline Workbook. Put your scene ideas into the order the audience will experience them. This becomes the map for your screenplay draft.

How to use it

  • List each scene in story order.
  • Connect each scene to a journey step.
  • Keep each scene description short.
  • Combine scenes that do the same job.

Ask of every scene

Does something change? Does it reveal character? Does it move the story forward? Can the audience see or hear what happens?
