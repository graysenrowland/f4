About the festival

What we value

Films with strong moral character, meaningful storytelling, & clear creative intent.

Submission Criteria

Films are evaluated on a stand-alone basis.
Collaboration is encouraged, and participants may contribute to multiple films in various roles.

Categories for submission

Feature Film

Narrative or films with a runtime of 30 minutes or more.

Short Film

Films under 30 minutes, including narrative, documentary, or experimental short-form work.

Documentary

Non-fiction films that inform, explore, investigate, or preserve meaningful real-world stories.

Awards

Official awards are determined by judges, with a separate audience award based on viewer ratings.

Best Feature Film Best Short Film Best Documentary Audience Choice

How films are evaluated

Story & Writing

Narrative strength, clarity, and emotional impact.

  • Story arc (setup, conflict, resolution)
  • Character development
  • Dialogue quality
  • Thematic depth

Audience rating

In addition to juried awards, F₄ includes an audience rating component so viewers can recognize films that genuinely connected with the room.

1–5 Simple rating scale

How it works

Attendees rate a film after its screening using a mobile-friendly form or QR code.

What it decides

The highest qualified audience score receives the Audience Choice Award.
